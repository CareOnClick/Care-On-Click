Sponsorships

Be a sponsor:
Care On Click is here to make sure that healthcare workers receive the care they need! All proceeds from our sponsors will go towards keeping our workers stress-free with our care packages goods. Check out Contact Us to inquire about how you can contribute!

Volunteer

Be a volunteer:
As a community based organization, volunteers are vital to CareOnClick’s caring activities. Without hard work and commitment of our core members, Care On Click wouldn’t have gotten this far! Let us know in Contact us to join the team!
