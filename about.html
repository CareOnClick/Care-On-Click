Our Purpose

Since the beginning of this pandemic, healthcare workers have been on the front lines risking their lives to ensure the safety and care of the public. Countless times, we have seen the struggles that they face, whether the lack of physical supplies or being mentally fatigued from their daily schedule. CareOnclick is a non-profit organization in development that is dedicated to supporting healthcare workers. From resources to care packages, CareOnClick provides easy access to support for the healthcare workers with just the click of a button. We provide an opportunity, for you, to show your support through donations or words of encouragement and take you inside the journey of fighting COVID-19.
